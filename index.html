About The KGM

The KGM is a food critique company largely active in the GTA exploring new restaurants from the high-end to the low-end and all the way around. It is run by the highly skilled and opinionated food tasters Khushi, Grace, and Memma who founded The KGM in 2021 inspired by their insatiable food cravings.

WEEK ONE: JANUARY 3, 2021

Cactus Club

Dish Name: Raincoast Greens

The creaminess of the avocado is the saving grace of the recipe! The well seasoned salmon and veggies would’ve been a delight if they were not so overcooked that it became hard to ignore. However, it is almost impossible to find a dish so well seasoned so I might give this another go and hope it was a one time error.

Address: 25 The West Mall Unit 700, Toronto, ON M9C 1B8

KŌST

Dish Name: Ricotta Agnolotti

This is hands-down one of the best spinach and ricotta agnolotti I have ever tasted. From the freshness of the ricotta to the decadence of the agnolotti, this was a mouth pleasing dish. Depending on your tastebuds, this may be a bit bland so make sure you tell your chef for seasoning it more.

Address: 280 Blue Jays Way 44th Floor, Toronto, ON M5V 2G3

Earls Kitchen + Bar

Dish Name: Thai Chicken Curry Bowl
The chicken was good, the curry was good, the veggies were good, this dish was good. Not great. This dish is perfect for when you are not expecting much from your food and feeding yourself for the sake of it, don’t order this expecting you will be blown away.

Address: 100 City Centre Dr Unit 1-101, Mississauga, ON L5B 2C9

Vegan Villa

Dish Name: Avocado Pepper Bowl
The bowl was good, the veggies were good, this dish was great. Overall, this is highly recommended.

Address: 300 City Centre Dr Unit 1-101, Mississauga, ON L5B 2C9
